About Pearl Global

Modern mushroom cultivation with deep respect for nature and food safety.

Pearl Global is a dedicated mushroom farm focused on supplying fresh, high‑quality edible mushrooms for homes, retailers and restaurants. Our controlled indoor farms maintain ideal temperature, humidity and hygiene for safe and consistent harvests.

From carefully prepared substrate to gentle hand‑picking, every step is optimized to preserve nutrition, flavour and shelf life. Only food‑grade mushrooms are grown: no decorative or non‑edible species.

How We Grow

A clean, science‑backed process that keeps every batch safe and tasty.

1. Spawn & Substrate

High‑quality spawn and carefully prepared substrate are sterilized for contamination‑free growth.

2. Incubation

Mycelium colonizes the substrate in a clean, temperature‑controlled incubation room.

3. Fruiting

Optimized humidity, airflow and lighting trigger healthy mushroom fruiting in our grow rooms.

4. Harvest & Delivery

Fresh mushrooms are hand‑picked, packed and dispatched quickly so you receive them at peak freshness.
